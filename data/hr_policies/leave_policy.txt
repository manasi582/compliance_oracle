Leave Policy
------------

Objective:
To define the various types of leaves available to employees and ensure consistency in leave approvals and utilization.

Eligibility:
All permanent employees are eligible for leave benefits from the date of joining.

Types of Leave:
1. **Casual Leave (CL):**
   - 12 days per calendar year.
   - Intended for personal matters or emergencies.
   - Cannot be carried forward.

2. **Earned Leave (EL):**
   - 15 days per year.
   - Can be carried forward up to 30 days.
   - Encashment available during exit or year-end, subject to approval.

3. **Sick Leave (SL):**
   - 10 days per year.
   - A medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds 2 consecutive days.

4. **Maternity / Paternity Leave:**
   - As per government guidelines and employment contract terms.

5. **Unpaid Leave:**
   - If leave balance is exhausted, additional days will be considered as unpaid.

Leave Application Process:
- All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 3 days in advance.
- Emergency leaves should be informed to the reporting manager at the earliest.

Holidays:
The list of national and festival holidays will be published by HR at the start of each year.

Non-Compliance:
Failure to report repeated unauthorized absence may lead to disciplinary action.
