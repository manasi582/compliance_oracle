Dress Code Policy
-----------------

Objective:
To maintain a professional work environment and ensure employees represent the company appropriately to clients and partners.

Scope:
Applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees working on company premises or attending client meetings.

General Guidelines:
- Employees are expected to wear neat, clean, and business-appropriate clothing during working hours.
- Clothing with offensive or inappropriate graphics, language, or symbols is not permitted.
- Footwear must be clean and appropriate for the office environment.

Business Attire:
- On regular weekdays (Mondayâ€“Thursday), employees must wear business casuals or formal attire.
- For client meetings and presentations, formal business wear is required unless stated otherwise by management.

Casual Fridays:
- Fridays are designated as casual dress days. Employees may wear jeans and casual shirts while maintaining neatness and decency.

Remote Employees:
- Remote employees should maintain a professional appearance during video calls and meetings.

Non-Compliance:
Repeated violations of the dress code may lead to a verbal or written warning from HR.
