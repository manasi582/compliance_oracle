Resignation and Exit Policy
---------------------------

Objective:
To ensure a transparent and organized process for employee separation, whether voluntary or involuntary.

Resignation Procedure:
- Employees must submit a formal resignation letter or email to their reporting manager and HR.
- The standard notice period is 30 days unless otherwise stated in the employment contract.

Notice Period Buyout:
- Employees may request a notice period buyout, subject to managerial approval and deduction of salary equivalent to unserved days.

Exit Interviews:
- HR will conduct an exit interview to understand reasons for departure and collect employee feedback.
- Constructive feedback is documented and shared anonymously with management.

Clearance Process:
- All company property (laptops, ID cards, access cards, etc.) must be returned before the final working day.
- Employees must obtain clearance from their department heads.

Final Settlement:
- The final paycheck, including remaining salary, bonus, and encashed leave, will be processed within 45 days of the last working day.

Rehire Eligibility:
- Employees leaving in good standing may be considered for re-employment in the future.

Policy Violations:
- Failure to serve the notice period or return company assets may lead to withholding of full and final settlement until compliance.
