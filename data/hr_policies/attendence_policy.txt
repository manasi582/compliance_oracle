Attendance and Punctuality Policy
---------------------------------

Objective:
To ensure consistent attendance and punctuality across all departments and to maintain workplace discipline.

Working Hours:
- Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Employees are required to log in and log out through the HRMS system daily.

Grace Period:
- A grace period of 10 minutes is permitted for morning arrival.
- Repeated tardiness (more than 3 times in a month) may result in a written warning.

Leaves and Absences:
- Planned leaves must be applied for at least 3 days in advance.
- Uninformed absences exceeding 2 consecutive working days may lead to salary deduction or disciplinary action.

Half-Day / Short Leave:
- Employees may take a half-day up to twice a month with prior manager approval.
- Excessive short leaves will be treated as full-day absences.

Work from Home Attendance:
- Remote employees must mark attendance digitally via the HR portal and remain reachable during working hours.

Records and Accountability:
- Attendance records will be reviewed monthly by HR.
- Consistent attendance is a key component of performance evaluation.
