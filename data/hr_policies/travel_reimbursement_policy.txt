Travel and Reimbursement Policy
-------------------------------

Objective:
To define the process for travel expense approvals and reimbursements incurred for official business trips.

Scope:
Applies to all employees who travel for client meetings, training, conferences, or other business purposes.

Pre-Approval:
- All travel must be pre-approved by the reporting manager and HR.
- Employees must submit a travel request form with estimated costs and itinerary.

Eligible Expenses:
- Airfare, train, or bus tickets (economy class)
- Local transportation (cab, metro, auto)
- Accommodation at company-approved hotels
- Meals during travel within prescribed limits
- Internet and communication charges for business use

Non-Reimbursable Expenses:
- Alcohol, personal entertainment, and leisure activities
- Room service or minibar costs
- Fines or penalties during travel

Submission Process:
- Employees must submit all original receipts and a travel expense form within 7 working days after returning.
- Reimbursement will be processed within 10 working days after approval.
