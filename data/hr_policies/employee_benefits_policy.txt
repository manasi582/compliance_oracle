Employee Benefits Policy
------------------------

Objective:
To outline the benefits provided by the company to support employee well-being and retention.

Scope:
Applies to all permanent full-time employees. Contractual or part-time staff may receive prorated benefits as per contract terms.

Health Insurance:
- The company provides comprehensive medical insurance for all employees and their immediate family members.
- Coverage includes hospitalization, maternity benefits, and preventive health check-ups.

Paid Leaves:
- Full-time employees are entitled to annual, sick, and casual leaves as defined under the Leave Policy.

Provident Fund and Gratuity:
- All employees are enrolled in the company’s provident fund as per applicable labor laws.
- Gratuity benefits apply after completion of 5 years of continuous service.

Wellness & Mental Health:
- Employees have access to mental wellness programs, therapy sessions, and health workshops organized quarterly.

Employee Assistance:
- Financial and legal advisory services are provided through the HR’s Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

Learning & Development:
- The company reimburses 50% of costs for job-related certification or training approved by management.

Policy Review:
Benefits are reviewed annually and subject to change based on business conditions or updated government regulations.
