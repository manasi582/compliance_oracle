Information Security & Data Protection Training

All employees are responsible for safeguarding company information and digital assets.

1. Passwords & Authentication  
   - Use unique, strong passwords with at least 12 characters.  
   - Enable multi-factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible.  

2. Email & Phishing Awareness  
   - Never click links or attachments from unknown senders.  
   - Forward suspicious emails to security@company.com.

3. Device & Network Security  
   - Lock your system when unattended.  
   - Only use company-approved cloud storage.  
   - Remote employees must use the VPN when connecting to internal systems.

4. Data Handling  
   - Store sensitive data only on encrypted drives.  
   - Delete unused confidential files regularly.

5. Incident Response  
   - Immediately report suspected breaches to the IT Security team.  
   - Follow provided recovery and reset procedures.

Failure to comply may result in disciplinary action or access suspension.
