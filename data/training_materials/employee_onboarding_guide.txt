Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to our organization! This document will help you settle into your new role quickly and confidently.

1. Orientation & Company Overview  
   - Attend the virtual onboarding session within your first week.  
   - Review the company mission, values, and core culture principles.

2. IT Setup Checklist  
   - Collect your system credentials from IT support.  
   - Enroll your device in the company’s endpoint protection software.  
   - Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all accounts.

3. HR & Compliance Documents  
   - Submit all signed HR forms within 48 hours of joining.  
   - Complete the “Code of Conduct” e-module and “Security Awareness” training within 10 days.

4. Mentorship & Buddy Program  
   - You’ll be paired with a senior colleague for guidance during your first 90 days.  

5. Important Contacts  
   - HR Support: hr@company.com  
   - IT Helpdesk: support@company.com  
   - Security Team: security@company.com
