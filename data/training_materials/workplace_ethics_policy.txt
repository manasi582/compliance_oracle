Workplace Ethics & Professional Conduct Policy

Our company is committed to maintaining a fair, transparent, and respectful work environment.

1. Respect & Integrity  
   - Treat all employees and partners with dignity.  
   - Avoid discrimination or harassment in any form.

2. Conflict of Interest  
   - Avoid personal or financial relationships that could influence professional decisions.  
   - Disclose potential conflicts to HR immediately.

3. Confidentiality  
   - Protect all company and customer data.  
   - Do not share sensitive documents outside authorized channels.

4. Whistleblower Protection  
   - Report unethical or illegal activities anonymously via ethics@company.com.  
   - Retaliation against whistleblowers is strictly prohibited.

5. Professionalism  
   - Maintain punctuality, teamwork, and accountability.  
   - Represent the company positively in all professional contexts.
